## 1. [Wavelengths and Energy Levels of Neutral Manganese (Mn I) Determined Using High-Resolution Fourier Transform and Grating Spectroscopy](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.15521) <a id="link1"></a>

**ArXiv ID:** 2511.15521
