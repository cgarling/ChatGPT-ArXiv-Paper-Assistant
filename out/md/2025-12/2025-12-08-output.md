## 1. [Stability of the Milky Way Satellite Galaxy Plane under the Influence of Neighbors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.05801) <a id="link1"></a>

**ArXiv ID:** 2512.05801

**Authors:** S. V. Pilipenko, N. R. Arakelyan
