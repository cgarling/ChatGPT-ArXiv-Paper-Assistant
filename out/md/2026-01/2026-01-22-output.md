## 1. [Extra modes in helium-core-burning stars probing an infra core cavity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.14507) <a id="link1"></a>

**Authors:** B. Mosser, M. Takata, C. Pin\c{c}on, M. S. Cunha, M. Vrard, K. Belkacem, S. Deheuvels, M. Matteuzzi

**Abstract:** Dipole mixed modes observed in the oscillation pattern of red giant stars probe the radiative regions in the stellar core.
